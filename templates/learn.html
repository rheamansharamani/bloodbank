Blood Types & Compatibility

Blood is classified into different types based on the presence or absence of certain antigens. The main blood groups are A, B, AB, and O, and each can be either Rh-positive (+) or Rh-negative (−).

💡 Fun Facts

  • Only 7% of the population has O− blood, but it's the most needed for emergencies.
  • AB+ is the rarest blood type, found in less than 4% of people.
  • Knowing your blood type is important in case of surgeries, transfusions, or emergencies.
